Reading this board: rows are the listing (seller-side) broker — the gatekeeper to call. A PENDING badge means contingencies are removed (near-certain to close) and is highlighted with a purple bar; UNDER CONTRACT means an offer is accepted but contingencies may still be live. The buyer's-side agent is never published on public listings — that requires an MLS/RESO feed (see docs/FEASIBILITY_AND_SOLUTIONS.md). Broker phone/email are pulled from the listing where the source exposes them; blanks mean the source hid the email behind a contact form. Your CRM contact cross-references the Monday.com All Contacts board: listing agent = the CRM person IS the listing agent (matched by name — their email/phone also fills the broker column); @ brokerage = your relationship at that brokerage, a warm door in, but NOT necessarily the agent on this deal. Why most rows say landwatch: LandWatch is the one big source with a public under-contract filter. Land Broker MLS and Hall and Hall hide pending deals, so their inventory sits in the "Active tracked" pool — when one of their listings vanishes, it appears here as Disappeared — probable UC with the broker contact already captured. Multi-source deals show all sources (e.g. landwatch+landbrokermls). Click any column header to sort.
